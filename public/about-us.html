Our Mission

We are students of GMIT, passionate about creating a positive impact on campus. Our mission is to support students by fostering mental wellness and social connections through technology.

Through MindfulConnect, we aim to build a supportive community where students can find resources, connect with peers, and prioritize their mental health during their academic journey.

Our Journey

How MindfulConnect came to be and our vision for the future

The Beginning

September 2024

MindfulConnect started as a class project when we noticed the need for better mental health resources on campus.

Research Phase

October 2024

We conducted surveys and interviews with students to understand their needs and challenges.

Platform Development

November 2024

Our team began developing the platform, focusing on creating a safe and supportive online environment.

Beta Launch

January 2025

MindfulConnect was launched in beta to a small group of students for testing and feedback.

Official Launch

March 2025

After incorporating feedback and making improvements, we officially launched MindfulConnect to the entire GMIT community.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where every student feels supported, connected, and empowered to prioritize their mental wellbeing. We aim to expand MindfulConnect to other educational institutions and continue improving our platform based on student feedback.
